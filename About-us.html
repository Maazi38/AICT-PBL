About CAKETROPIA

Founded in Islamabad in 2021, the philosophy of Caketropia Bakeshop was smiple; to create connection through cakes while striking the ideal balance between flavours and feeling.Since the begning, Caketropia has secured an irreplaceable spot in the hearts of the people.Being the quintessential representation of aesthetics and taste, a Caketropian cake is consumed by the eyes well before delighting the tastebuds.

Growing & Expanding

The love for Caketropia has only blossomed with time. We now have 32 stores in different locations including Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Faisalabad, Sialkot and Gujranwala, and this is just the beginning.

Our dream to bake the best desserts in town continues to flourish, and we are over the moon to have created a sweet utopia for dessert lovers to make their sweet escape from reality to fantasy!

Our experts care for your taste buds and pour their efforts in making rich, creamy taste, and aesthetically beautiful texture in cakes.

Feel Free to ask questions just Contact Us here!

Caketropia is aiming the disrupt the market and demand of the cakes in Pakistan with our delectable, decadent sweet flavor, and irresistible bakery items.
